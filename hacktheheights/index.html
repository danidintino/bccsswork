About the Event

We are excited to introduce Boston College's first full-scale hackathon! Hack the Heights will feature corporate sponsors, tech talks, and prizes for beginners and seasoned hackers alike. Hacking will begin Friday, April 14 at 11am and conclude Saturday, April 15 at 12pm.

Interested in learning more? See the FAQ below or reach out to bccssociety@gmail.com with any questions.
